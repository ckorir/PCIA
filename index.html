About Us

Prime Cover Insurance Agency Limited is a firm with top notch capabilities committed to being an agency of first choice for clients who demand the best in exceptional service delivery and personalized expertise. Primarily, we help our clients to identify their insurance needs, and buy the needed insurance on behalf of our clients on the most favourable terms and price. We are also responsible for the processing and negotiation of claims under the insurances we buy for our clients.
